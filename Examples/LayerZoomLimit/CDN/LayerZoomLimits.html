Layer Zoom Limits

Layers support minZoom and maxZoom options for controlling visibility based on the view's zoom level.
If min or max zoom are set, the layer will only be visible at zoom levels greater than the minZoom and less than or equal to the maxZoom.
After construction, the layer's setMinZoom and setMaxZoom can be used to set limits.
